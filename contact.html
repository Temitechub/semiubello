Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What services do you offer?

I offer health communication research, consultancy services, academic training, and project evaluation for NGOs, government agencies, and academic institutions.

How can I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form above or sending an email to semiubello@example.com. I typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Do you accept speaking engagements?

Yes, I accept speaking engagements for conferences, workshops, and seminars related to health communication, media research, and public health. Please provide details about your event in the contact form.

Can you supervise research students?

Yes, I supervise graduate and postgraduate students in communication, health communication, and media studies. Contact me to discuss potential research topics and supervision availability.
